Briards

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briards, or berge de brie, is a large breed of dog traditional used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is belivedto have orginated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries,messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing.Briards were used in First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of braids is slowly recovering Charlemagne, Napolean, Thomas Jefferson and lafayette all owned briards.
